Frequently Asked Questions

How does the weekly payment work?

You pay 10,000 MWK per week. Each payment gives you access to all 9 subjects for that week. You can pay for multiple weeks at once to get discounts.

What happens when my access expires?

Your access will automatically expire at the end of your paid period. You'll need to make another payment to continue. All your progress is saved and will be available when you renew.

Can I download the lessons?

For copyright protection, lessons are streamed online and cannot be downloaded. However, all PDF notes and worksheets are available for download during your access period.

How do I apply for the Three Fold Bursary?

After registering, go to Payment Options and select "Three Fold Bursary". You'll need to fill out an application form and provide supporting documents. Applications are reviewed within 5 working days.

What if I have payment problems?

Contact our payment support team at payments@threefoldventures.com or WhatsApp +265 888 123 456. We're available Monday to Saturday, 8 AM to 6 PM.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available within 24 hours of payment if you haven't accessed any content. After accessing content, refunds are at the discretion of management and require a valid reason.
